Internships

During my time as a Computer Vision Research Intern at IIT Madras under the Summer Fellowship Program, I focused on Generalizable NeRF Transformers (GNT), gaining hands-on experience with 3D scene rendering and manipulation. My primary task was to enhance GNT by incorporating depth information, aiming to improve the accuracy of 3D renderings. This involved delving deep into the intricacies of 3D scene reconstruction, applying advanced computer vision techniques to produce more precise and realistic visual outputs. The internship provided me with valuable exposure to cutting-edge research in 3D computer vision and further refined my technical and research skills.

During my time as an AI Intern at Pi Datacenters, I developed AI-powered chatbots using large language models (LLMs), LangChain, and Langflow, enhancing my skills in natural language processing and machine learning. I was involved in the creation of a website support agent that utilized Flask, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for secure authentication and seamless user interaction. Additionally, I worked on integrating web scraping techniques and the Pinecone database to enable efficient search and storage capabilities. I also contributed to building customized chatbots for specific use cases, such as personal document handling, interview agents, and general query responses, employing ChromaDB and Dash to improve the interactivity and functionality of the bots.

At Valsco Technology, I worked as a Frontend Web Development Intern, collaborating with the frontend team to design and develop the ‘About Us’ and ‘FAQ’ pages for the company’s website. I utilized React.js, CSS, and JavaScript to create responsive, user-friendly layouts that were optimized for cross-browser and cross-device compatibility. My contributions helped improve the overall user experience and performance of the website. I also assisted in ensuring that the site was accessible and functional on various platforms, further honing my frontend development skills and gaining insights into best practices for web design and optimization.
